Hey, I'm Keegan! I'm a Clinical Robot Technician, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction. I've worked on numerous projects ranging from web design using HTML/CSS and JavaScript and programming Android apps and SQL databases, to UX/UI work, designing for accessibility with community and state-level stakeholders.

Professional Background

I graduated from the University of Washington with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Innovation & Design. There, I gained experience working in teams and individually to meet technical spcifications and user needs. My nontraditional approach to Computer Science has allowed me to approach problems with user-centric design principles and implementing those ideas with technological skills. My professional experience also includes working in multiple fast-paced, customer-focused environments for almost 8 years. Much of that work has beeen in a supervisory role, where I've learned administrative and leadership skills. I currently work in hospitals, where I monitor and troubleshoot autonomous delivery robots and work with clients to ensure their needs are met.

Personal Background

I was born and raised in Olympia, Washington, where I grew up reading and playing baseball and lacrosse. In high school, I joined an after-school coding club, where I learned the basics and found my direction for college. I've always enjoyed working with my hands and thinking creatively, and pursuing my minor in Innovation & Design has allowed me to explore those interests in conjunction with learning software development. I also love the outdoors that Washington offers, and on a day off I can be found on a lake, camping, or snowboarding!
